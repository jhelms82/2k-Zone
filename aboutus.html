About Us

The NBA 2K Zone League is a 2K Sim-League that aims for members with a place to enjoying playing a game we all love. We are not your basic 2K toxic community that some have a reputation for.

THe 2k Zone accepts players who are looking for a pay comp league while being respective to each other. For great players that want a comp league, while having a great group of guys to chill with, the 2K Zone league is the right place for you.

We have no affiliation with 2K or the NBA.

We exist to serve our community and will continue to offer services and competitive eSport events the NBA 2K Zone League community desires.
